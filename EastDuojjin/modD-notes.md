## Consonant stuff

The consonant inventory of ND is:
````
Stops:       p t d k ɡ
Nasals:      m n        ʔn
Liquids:     ɾ l           j
Fricatives:  v s z         ç- ʃ ʒ h
````
where /n/ can be syllabic.
